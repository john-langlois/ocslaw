Services

At OCS Law, we offer a comprehensive suite of legal services designed to meet the diverse needs of businesses and organizations across various industries. Osvaldo is well experienced and committed to providing strategic guidance and legal solutions that support your business goals. OCS Law services include:

Business Formation and Restructuring

Our firm assists in the formation of various business entities, including corporations, partnerships, LLCs, and sole proprietorships, ensuring that you choose the optimal structure for your business's legal and operational needs.

International Business

We offer guidance on international trade, cross-border transactions, and global expansion, addressing legal and regulatory challenges associated with international business operations.

Contract Drafting and Review

We offer expertise in drafting, reviewing, and negotiating contracts and agreements of all types, including commercial contracts, partnership agreements, and shareholder agreements, to protect your interests and minimize legal risks.

Contract Negotiation

We provide legal counsel to clients engaging in contract negotiations, ensuring that agreements are legally sound, compliant with relevant laws and regulations, and aligned with our clients' objectives.

Contract Mediation

Our firm facilitates the resolution of disputes through a legally guided process. We assist parties in finding mutually acceptable solutions, promoting compliance with legal requirements, and mitigating the need for adversarial litigation.

Commercial Transactions

We advise on a wide range of commercial transactions, including asset and stock purchases, joint ventures, licensing agreements, and distribution agreements, to facilitate successful business transactions.

Alternative Dispute Resolution

Our firm is specialized in alternative dispute resolution methods, including mediation and arbitration, to resolve conflicts efficiently and cost-effectively.

Corporate Governance

We provide guidance on corporate governance practices, helping businesses maintain compliance with regulatory requirements and establish transparent and accountable management structures.

Mergers and Acquisitions

Our firm guides clients through the complexities of mergers, acquisitions, and divestitures, from due diligence to negotiation and transaction closing, ensuring a seamless transition and favorable outcomes.

Employment and Labor Law

We provide comprehensive counsel on employment and labor law matters, including employment contracts, employee handbooks, non-compete agreements, wage and hour compliance, and workplace dispute resolution.

Real Estate Transactions

We handle all aspects of real estate transactions, including property acquisitions, leasing, zoning, land use matters, and real estate development projects.

Wills and Estate

Our law firm specializes in wills and estate matters, providing clients with essential services to plan for the future and manage estates. We offer assistance in creating legally valid wills, strategic estate planning, probate, estate administration, trust establishment, and representation in estate litigation. Additionally, we provide professional executor and trustee services, and comprehensive estate tax planning to protect and preserve family wealth.
